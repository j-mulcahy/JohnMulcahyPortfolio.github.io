Erma 712 Rifle

A low poly game ready asset of a Erma 712 Lever Action Long Rifle with working internals. I wanted to try my hand at sculpting some filigree in Zbrush and saw this as a perfect opportunity. LP Moddeled in 3ds Max, HPs done in Zbrush, textured in Substance Painter.
