Stylized Jade Amulet

This is a real time asset rendered in UE4. Original concept is by Roberto Gatto, his stuff is super cool and you can find his work here: artstation.com/robertogatto

This amulet is a take on the original amulet that was designed for Oblivion. 5.5K Tris (Without fibers), LP done in 3Ds Max, HP done in Zbrush, Lodded in Maya, Textured in Substance.
