Cruiser Skateboard

Here's a somewhat quick project I working on. I Wanted to create something simple but also something that could be used to enhance my texturing skills. LP game ready cruiser skateboard. Final tri-count is 14,762. I also took this opportunity to look into marmoset render in more depth too. All renders are From Marmoset Toolbag. Modeled in max - HPs in Zbrush - Textured in Substance, alphas and decals such as stickers we're optimized in Photoshop. Original Deck Artwork by Shang Chengxiang.
