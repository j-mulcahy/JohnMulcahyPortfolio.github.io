Hi there!

Welcome to my Portfolio! I hope you found it easy to navigate, this was my first venture into HTML code, and it was .....interesting. Anyway, enough about that, allow me to introduce you myself.

I am a 3D Artist with an interest in Hard Surface Modelling. I have a plethora of skills that include but are not limited to realistic texturing, modelling, sculpting, rigging, animating, material creation and game engine implementation. Aside from making games, I love to play them, and have ever since I can remember. I don't think I could ever pick a favourite but Santa Monica Studios are making it hard to not with their new God of War titles. Outside of gaming, I would say I'm also an avid podcast listener and I can make a mean banana bread!
