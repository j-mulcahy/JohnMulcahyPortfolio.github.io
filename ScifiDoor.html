Sci-fi Door

This a personal project I worked on for about a week, fully realtime SCI-Fi door, I tried to re-create a 'Dead Space' Vibe. Dynamically lit in UE4, with animations. Animated and Modeled in 3DSMax, HP's done in Zbrush & Textured in Substance Painter. Final Tri-count is 13,473.
